About Me

I'm a third-year Computer Science (Software Technology) student at Universiti Kebangsaan Malaysia (UKM) with a strong interest in software development, mobile applications and database systems. Before pursuing my degree, I completed my Foundation in Science at UiTM Dengkil and previously studied Pure Science at Sekolah Menengah Sains Dungun (SEMESDI).

Throughout my degree journey, I’ve worked on various academic and personal projects involving Android development, real-time systems, database integration and web technologies while continuously exploring new tools and improving my technical skills.

Beyond academics, I’m also a Yayasan Telekom Malaysia YTM Future Leaders Scholar and someone who enjoys learning beyond the classroom. I also spent part of my semester break managing a small business, which helped me develop communication, teamwork and customer service skills beyond the tech field.
